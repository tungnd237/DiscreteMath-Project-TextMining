    Background knowledge

    Text mining is gaining useful information from text which is mostly developed by Artificial Intelligence (AI). By transforming the data into a more structured format through text mining and text analysis, more quantitative insights can be found through text analytics. It enables researchers to analyze massive amounts of information quickly. The results of mining can surface important links between entities that may not have been found otherwise. When researching, we come to the question that whether machine learning is the best way to do text mining. We came to an answer that we believe graph theory could also help us with text mining in a relatively more efficient way.

    What we do

    In this project, we will be using graph theory to mining the data from the first page of vnexpress (a famous newspaper in Vietnam) news to day. It will take all the headlines of the newspaper that day and output the most useful paper that we think that covers the most important information. In that way, you do not have to read through too many news and headlines that are basically same.
